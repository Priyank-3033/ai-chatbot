# Customer Support Knowledge Base

## Account Access
Customers can reset their password from the login page by clicking "Forgot Password".
Password reset links expire after 30 minutes for security reasons.
If a customer no longer has access to their email inbox, a support agent must manually verify identity before updating the email address.

## Billing and Refunds
Monthly subscriptions renew automatically at the start of each billing cycle.
Customers can download invoices from the Billing section of the dashboard.
Refund requests are eligible within 14 days of the last charge for annual plans, provided usage is below the fair-use threshold.

## Shipping and Orders
Standard shipping takes 3 to 5 business days.
Expedited shipping takes 1 to 2 business days.
Customers receive a tracking link by email once the order has been packed.
Orders can only be modified before they reach the packed state.

## Troubleshooting Login Issues
If a customer sees repeated "invalid credentials" errors, first confirm caps lock is off and the correct workspace is selected.
If multi-factor authentication fails, ask the customer to resync their authenticator app or request backup codes.
If the issue persists, collect the timestamp, browser, and screenshot before escalating.

## Escalation Policy
Escalate to a human agent when a question involves legal disputes, security incidents, refunds outside policy, or account ownership conflicts.
The chatbot should never invent policy exceptions.
